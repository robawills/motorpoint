Which car finance option is right for me?

Choose from one of our car finance options, both of which can be tailored to suit your needs and your circumstances.

Hire Purchase

Finance your car by spreading the cost with fixed regular monthly repayments, after which you own the car outright.

This type of finance is ideal if…

  • You want to have the full amount borrowed paid off by the end of agreement
  • You know you want to own the car outright at the end of the agreement
  • You don’t want to commit to monthly mileage limits

Personal Contract Purchase

Keep your monthly repayments lower by deferring a large proportion of credit into an optional final repayment (guaranteed minimum future value).

This is right for you if...

  • You want to keep your monthly payments as low as possible
  • You’re already planning to exchange the car for a new one at the end of the period
  • You’re confident about being able to commit to an agreed monthly mileage limit

Your car finance questions answered

How can I calculate my finance options?

We have a finance calculator on every car advert page. This will allow you to explore your repayment options for that car based on differing deposit amounts and repayment periods.

How can I apply for finance?

You can apply for finance either in-store or over the phone. Our teams will help you create a plan that meets your needs, answer any questions about any options you may have, and then complete the application for you.

What information do you require when applying for finance?

When you apply, we will require employment details, three years' address history, bank account details and a copy of your driving licence.

How long does it take to get a decision on finance?

The decision is normally instant once the proposal has been received by one of Motorpoint’s finance partners.

Can I settle my finance early?

You can settle your account at any time on both Hire Purchase (HP) and Personal Contract Purchase (PCP) agreements.
