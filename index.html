Which car finance option is right for me?

Choose from one of our car finance options, both of which can be tailored to suit your needs and your circumstances.

Hire Purchase

Finance your car by spreading the cost with fixed regular monthly repayments, after which you own the car outright.

This type of finance is ideal if…

  • You want to have the full amount borrowed paid off by the end of agreement
  • You know you want to own the car outright at the end of the agreement
  • You don’t want to commit to monthly mileage limits

Personal Contract Purchase

Keep your monthly repayments lower by deferring a large proportion of credit into an optional final repayment (guaranteed minimum future value).

This is right for you if...

  • You want to keep your monthly payments as low as possible
  • You’re already planning to exchange the car for a new one at the end of the period
  • You’re confident about being able to commit to an agreed monthly mileage limit
